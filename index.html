Who can get an e-Shram card?

expand_more

Any unorganized sector worker (like laborers, street vendors, domestic help, drivers, farmers, etc.) aged 16-59 who is not an income tax payer or EPF/ESIC member is eligible.

What are the benefits of e-Shram Card?

expand_more

It provides ₹2 Lakh free accidental insurance, cashless health cover under PMJAY, guaranteed monthly pension of ₹3,000 under PM-SYM after 60, and direct benefits from various welfare schemes.

Do I need to renew the card every year?

expand_more

No, the card is valid for life. There is no renewal fee or process. You only update it if your bank details, address, or mobile number change.
